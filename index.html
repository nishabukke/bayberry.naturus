About Us

In today's world, where environmental regulations are becoming increasingly stringent, the use of common industrial solvents for extraction is facing scrutiny due to their hazardous nature and potential risks to human health. As a result, SCFE technologies employing supercritical CO2 as a solvent have gained popularity and witnessed significant growth.SCCFE has earned its accepted status as Generally Recognized as Safe (GRAS), providing peace of mind for both consumers and industries.
